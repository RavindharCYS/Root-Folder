FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our consultation process

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, your 30-minute strategy session is completely free with no obligations. We believe in demonstrating our expertise and providing value upfront.

How should I prepare for the consultation?

To get the most value, consider your primary digital marketing goals, challenges you're facing, and any specific questions you'd like addressed. Having access to your current analytics or campaign data can also be helpful, but it's not required.

Who will I be speaking with?

You'll be connected with a senior specialist who has expertise in your specific area of interest. All our consultants have at least 5 years of experience in their respective fields.

How is the consultation conducted?

Consultations are conducted via Zoom video conference. After booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with the meeting link and calendar invitation. You'll also receive a reminder email 24 hours before your scheduled time.

What happens after the consultation?

After the call, we'll send you a summary of our discussion, including key takeaways and recommended next steps. If there's a good fit for working together, we'll include a proposal outlining potential options. However, there's never any pressure or obligation to move forward.

Can I reschedule if needed?

Yes, you can reschedule your appointment up to 4 hours before the scheduled time. Simply click the reschedule link in your confirmation email, or contact us at support@digitalnexus.com.
